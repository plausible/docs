---
title: Invoice email and billing access
---

Plausible and Paddle use separate email addresses. Your Plausible email is for logging in and viewing your stats. Your Paddle email is where invoices are sent.

## Change your invoice email

If you want invoices sent to a different address, [contact us](https://plausible.io/contact) with your current Paddle email and the new address you'd like to use. We'll update it with Paddle on your behalf.

To change your Plausible login email instead, see [change your account email](change-email.md).

## Give your accounting team billing access

You can add a team member with the Billing role. A Billing member can view and download all past invoices and manage your subscription and payment details directly within Plausible.

To add a billing member, go to your team settings and invite them with the Billing role. See [users and roles](users-roles.md) for details.
